Upon entering the finca there is a car park for several cars and a mature garden very well maintained. Upon entering the house there is a covered terrace that is divided into two areas, a living room and an area ideal for barbecues, from the covered terrace you can access the interior of the house where we find a charming kitchen with wooden beams on the roof, from the kitchen you access to a large living room with a nice fireplace and wooden beams on the roof, through a hallway you access a large bedroom with an ensuite bathroom, and dressing area, together to this bedroom there is a large study, which has a library and a fireplace, which can be converted into a large bedroom. This part of the house was an old mill, so it has a lot of charm.

From the living room of the house you can access the new part of the house, which consists of a nice living room with wooden beams and fireplace, a fully equipped kitchen, two bedrooms with private terraces and a bathroom, this part of The house can be rented or it can be like a guest house, as it has a separate entrance.

There is a south facing pool with many hours of sun, surrounded by a stone terrace, from where you have a beautiful view of the sea and mountains.

It is a very private estate, which enjoys very nice views of the sea and mountains, the house has a lot of charm and has a mature garden very well maintained.

This property is located at 15 minutes by car to the beach and Estepona city, and 60 minutes to Malaga airport.
