About us

Began working in Spain in 1999, we are still here! We offer great value properties and even greater customer service when touring your selected properties with us.

We have homes all over all country!

We try hard to be on top of our game and will always listen to your needs and budget requirements.

Why buy a property in Spain through us?

Because we know the properties best, having built and sold them. We can, therefore, find you the perfect property, or find you the ideal client for your property.

Because we make a long term commitment, caring for our clients beyond their purchase. Our objective is to meet and then exceed your expectations.

Because we manage your personal service requirements through our Customer Services department

Because we are always at your disposal through our offices which are open to the public in Avenida Tomás Pascual 6, La Quinta Golf. The multi-lingual staff of Sueño are visible and accessible.
